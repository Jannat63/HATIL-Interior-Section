The Challenge

Practical, Beautiful — and Within Budget

A young couple with two children in Dhanmondi had just moved into their first owned apartment. They wanted a fully furnished home that looked premium but felt genuinely livable — no showroom stiffness, just a warm, functional space their family could actually use every day.

Budget was a real constraint. At ৳12–18 lakh total, every taka had to count. The design needed to prioritise the rooms the family actually lived in — the living room, the children's bedroom, and the kitchen — while still delivering a cohesive, polished result throughout.

The clients also had young children, meaning furniture durability, edge safety, and easy-to-clean surfaces were just as important as aesthetics.

Our Approach

Budget-Smart. Family-Ready. No Compromises on Quality.

HATIL's team approached this project with a clear priority framework: spend more on the living room (the most-seen space) and the children's bedroom (the most heavily used), and apply smart, cost-effective selections in the master bedroom and dining area.

For the living room, a HATIL L-shaped fabric sofa in a neutral tone became the anchor — chosen for comfort, durability, and visual impact. A low-profile centre table and a wall-mounted TV unit in a light-toned engineered wood finish kept the space open and uncluttered.

The children's bedroom was fitted with HATIL's SmartFit system — combining a compact bunk bed with integrated study desks and overhead shelving. This freed floor space for play while giving both children dedicated study areas. All edges were finished with rounded profiles and soft-close hardware.

The modular kitchen used a simple L-shape layout with durable melamine-finish cabinets — easy to maintain, resistant to kitchen moisture, and still visually clean.
