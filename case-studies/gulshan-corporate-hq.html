The Challenge

A Brand-New HQ That Needs to Impress

A fast-growing technology firm was relocating their entire Dhaka operations to a 5,000 sq.ft Grade-A space in Gulshan-2. The brief: create a workspace that communicates professionalism and innovation to visiting clients while keeping 50 employees comfortable and productive.

The space needed to accommodate open workstations, four executive suites, a 20-seat boardroom, a client reception area, a server room, and a pantry — all within a strict 10-week deadline tied to the firm's lease commencement date.

Brand colours — deep navy and white — had to be woven into the interior without making it feel heavy or oppressive.

Our Approach

Zone-Based Design for Maximum Productivity

HATIL's commercial interior team divided the space into five clear zones: Executive Zone, Open Workspace, Client-Facing Zone (reception + boardroom), Support Zone (pantry, storage), and Technical Zone (server room).

Each zone was designed with its own atmosphere — the client-facing areas used premium HATIL executive furniture in walnut veneer finish with clean white walls, while the open workspace used lighter, height-adjustable desks with ergonomic chairs to encourage movement.

The boardroom was the centrepiece — a custom 20-seat conference table manufactured at HATIL's Savar plant, flanked by high-back HATIL executive chairs in navy fabric to echo the client's brand.
