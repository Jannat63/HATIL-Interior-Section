The Challenge

A Blank Canvas, a Tight Timeline

The clients — a family of four moving into a brand-new 3,200 sq.ft Bashundhara apartment — needed their entire home furnished and ready within 7 weeks before the school year started.

Every room had to feel cohesive while serving distinct purposes: a formal living room for guests, a relaxed family dining area, a master bedroom with walk-in wardrobe, two children's rooms with study areas, and a fully functional modular kitchen.

The additional challenge: the client wanted a warm, modern aesthetic — not the generic dark-wood look common in Dhaka apartments. They wanted something lighter, more contemporary, that still felt genuinely Bangladeshi.

Key Challenges
01
Furnish 3,200 sq.ft across 6 rooms within a strict 7-week deadline

Achieve a lighter, contemporary aesthetic — away from Dhaka's typical dark-wood look
03
Custom wardrobes, SmartFit study units, and modular kitchen all from a single supplier
Our Approach

Design-First. Manufacture-Direct.

HATIL's interior team began with a full site measurement and a two-hour briefing with the family. Within five days, a complete 3D visualization was presented — covering all rooms with furniture placement, colour palette, and material selection.

Because all furniture is manufactured at HATIL's own plant in Savar, we were able to begin production immediately upon client sign-off — no waiting for third-party vendors. The modular kitchen cabinetry, the custom wardrobes for the master bedroom, and the SmartFit study units for the children's rooms were all produced and delivered on schedule.
